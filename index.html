    About LIA.

    Lärande i Arbete (LIA) is an internship for students who studies at Teknikhögskolan.

    LIA is the workplace-based part of the education and is there to give the student a deeper insight and practically rooted competence. In the education, it is seen as a course with course objectives that should assessed on the basis of performance

    Learn more about LIA here.
    About Teknikhögskolan.

    Teknikhögskolan (TH) is a Yrkeshögskola. TH is part of Acade Media founded 1996. AcadeMedia is Sweden's largest training company. In addition to operations in Sweden, the company also runs preschools in Norway and Germany.

    About Yrkeshögskola.

    Yrkeshögskola (YH) is a 1-3 years customized education fully focused for the market. A YH program cannot start without the permission from the Government that confirms that this specific industry/program has the demand. The Government has to do this continously to meet the expectations.

    Benefits with a LIA?

    • The LIA can be a way to find new employees for your workplace.

    • As a supervisor, you get the opportunity to reflect and develop yourself as a person and coworker.

    • As a supervisor, you get the opportunity to find new inspiration in your everyday life and challenge yourself.

    • The student becomes an "extra resource" for your workplace.

    Does the student get salary?

    Yes, if you want to. #joke. No, it is completely free to receive an LIA student. The student gets CSN.
